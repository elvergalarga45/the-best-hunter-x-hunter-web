Overwatch

Overwatch Gameplay

Overwatch is an online team-based game generally played as a first-person shooter. The game features several different game modes, principally designed around squad-based combat with two opposing teams of six players each.

Reviews by players

I've been playing this game for about 3 years, ever since it had a free-to-play weekend back in 2017. It has become one of my biggest passion video games. It plays incredibly well and no one character is better than the others. It all comes down to your ability to work well with a team, as it does not matter how many kills you get on your own, but how you pull off the objectives. There are very few technical bugs (at least from what I've experienced) and Blizzard is usually very quick when it comes to updates, for better or worse. Since then, I've collected a ton of Overwatch merchandise and spent more money than I should meeting voice actors at various conventions. I know it sounds weird to say a video game has had a large impact on your life, but this one definitely has; I've been playing video games since I was 5. If you're looking for than just racking up the most kills in a death match (looking at you CoD), this game will give you a new challenge if you have never tried out a MOBA-style game. As a plus, the short films that Blizzard has made in the past are Pixel quality animated, showing they really put a lot of effort into the game. Yeah, there are some questionable buffs/nerfs to characters or gameplay now and then, but this is expected as a game grows. Right now, you should be able to pick this up pretty cheap and it will give you something fun until the second one comes out. Could not recommend this more.

-Cam D ⋆⋆⋆⋆⋆

I got this game ever since release and it was hands down the best FPS I have ever played. The game is centralised towards teamwork and cooperation, encouraging you to grab some friends and have fun. Although some updates brought some changes that were in mixed opinion, the game has nevertheless been as fun as when it was first released. The game is still worth playing as it still has an active player-base in comp and quick-play. I have played this game for over 4 years now and the game-play has not gone stale once. There are over 30 heroes to play and they are well balanced (well not all of them, looking at you Brigitte and Moira), and the designs are perfect. Blizzard always has some fresh maps, heroes and content to be released. Buying this game was definitely one of the important choices I have made in my life. In conclusion, Overwatch has changed my life in ways I can't describe and for that thank you Blizzard! If Overwatch 2 comes out I'm certain I will play it.

-Allen Bolina ⋆⋆⋆⋆⋆
